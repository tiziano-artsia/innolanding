About

With our global population projected to hit 10 billion by 2050, the urgency of sustainable, high-quality protein production is clear. The EU's protein deficit, importing 70% of protein crops and 90% of soybeans, amplifies the need for fresh, eco-friendly protein sources to serve diverse markets.

InnoProtein rises to this challenge by developing novel products for food, feed, and more. By tapping into overlooked protein sources like microalgae, fungi, bacteria, and insects, combined with advanced extraction techniques, we're driving solutions that shrink the EU's protein gap and environmental impact.

Our expert consortium introduces an eco-business model, validating both economic viability and social-environmental benefits. Embracing circular&zero waste approach, InnoProtein minimizes waste by converting biomass and production streams into bioplastics, biostimulants and fermentation medium bio-based energy sources

Join us in revolutionizing protein production, steering towards a sustainable future.
